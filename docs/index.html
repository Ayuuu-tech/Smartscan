SmartScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

SmartScan is a card wallet that lets you scan and store your bank, loyalty and visiting cards. Your card data lives on your device, not on our servers. Here is exactly what happens with your information.

Section 1

What we access

Camera Used only while you scan a card. Card images are processed on your device and discarded — never saved or uploaded.
Biometrics (fingerprint / face) Checked by your operating system to unlock the wallet. SmartScan never sees or stores your biometric data.
Contacts Accessed only when you tap “Save to contacts” on a scanned visiting card — to check for duplicates and add the one contact you choose. We never read your contacts otherwise.
Google account Used only to sign you in and, if you turn it on, to store your own encrypted backup file in your Google Drive.
Email address Used to send you a one-time verification code and, if requested, a password-reset link. Nothing else.
Section 2

Where your card data is stored

On your device only Card numbers, expiry dates and barcodes are kept in an encrypted vault backed by your phone’s secure hardware (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain). They are never uploaded to any server, including ours.
Encrypted backups (optional) If you export or enable automatic backup, the file is encrypted with a passphrase only you know. Without that passphrase the file is unreadable — even to us.
Autofill (Android) If you enable autofill, an encrypted, CVV-free copy of your payment cards stays on-device so Android can fill checkout forms you choose to fill.
Section 3

What we do not do

We never sell or rent your data Your information is not shared with advertisers or data brokers.
We never upload your card numbers They stay on your phone. There is no SmartScan server holding your cards.
We never process payments Payments happen in your own UPI app. SmartScan only pre-fills the details you enter.
Section 4

Your control

App lock Settings → Security. Require biometrics every time the app opens.
CVV storage Off by default. Enable it only if you want CVVs saved.
Delete anytime Deleting a card removes it permanently from the vault. Uninstalling the app erases the entire vault from your device.
